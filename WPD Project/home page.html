About University

Shivaji University, Kolhapur

Shivaji university established in 1962 , is in Kolhapur, Maharashtra , India. The university's campus is 853 acres (3.4519km2), and is named Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj , founder of maratha Empire. It was inaugurated on 18th November 1962 by Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan , the then President of India .Yashvantrao Chavan and Balasaheb Desai took the lead in establishing this university. Kolhapur, Sangli, and Satara come under its jurisdiction with 279 affiliatsd colleges and recognised institutes. One of the major objectives behind it's foundation was to cater to the regional neets of south Maharshtra.
